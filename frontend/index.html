🔍 Key Findings

Cropland dominance: With 68% of samples, Cropland achieves the highest F1 (0.96) due to large representation and distinct spectral signatures in Sentinel-2 imagery.
Built-up accuracy: 91% recall for urban areas demonstrates that the model effectively identifies man‑made infrastructure from satellite patches.
Class imbalance: Water (only 3 test samples) has perfect recall but low precision — weighted loss helps but more data is needed for robust Water classification.
Vegetation confusion: 10% of Vegetation patches are misclassified as Built-up, likely due to mixed urban-green interface pixels at the patch boundary.
